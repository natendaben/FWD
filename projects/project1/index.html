The Story Of

Nate and Leilani

This is the story of Nate Bennett and Leilani Osmundson, and how both of their lives inevitably came together.

Moved to CU Boulder

august 2016

Leilani and Nate moved into their new rooms at the University of Colorado in Boulder, both nervous and excited!

First Meeting

august 17, 2016

Nate put on his favorite turtle shirt and went to a Kickoff BBQ for his leadership program. His first college event! He met a girl named Leilani Osmundson, and they talked for a while about climbing Colorado's 14ers.

Bus Ride

august 21, 2016

On the bumpy bus ride down from their leadership orientation, Nate and Leilani ended up sitting next to each other and talking the entire way. When she got home, Leilani told her roommate that she was going to become best friends with this "Nate" kid.

All-Nighter

september 30, 2016

Just as Leilani had predicted, the two were quickly becoming fast friends. After doing some homework one night, they stayed up all night together talking about music, college, and life.

Mt. Elbert

october 8, 2016

On a camping trip with some friends, Leilani got altitude sickness while attempting to climb Mt. Elbert. Nate decided to stay with her to make sure she was okay, and he walked her down the mountain in the end.

Meeting the Parents

october 20, 2016

A few weeks later, Nate met Leilani's parents at her twin sister's violin concert. They thanked him for taking care of her.

Making the Move

january 17, 2017

After finally getting up the nerve, Nate asked Leilani on a date in front of Varsity Pond on a frosty winter night. She said yes! Now Nate was more nervous, because he had to plan a date.

First Date!

january 20, 2017

For their first date, they went to a fancy Italian restaurant on Pearl Street. After dinner, the two drove up Flagstaff mountain and hiked to an overlook of the city, where they set up a hammock. Nate tried to impress her by playing his ukulele and singing to her.

Dinner and War Clubs

february 24, 2017

Leilani's parents hosted the new couple for a steak dinner. Leilani's dad personally showed Nate their Samoan war club collection in the basement, just so he knew what he was getting himself into.

First Road Trip

march 25-30, 2017

With Leilani's sister Lina and another friend from college, Nate and Leilani went on their first road trip together for spring break. They backpacked and hiked around Utah and ate lots of good food.

Backpacking Trip

june 2017

Before Nate left to hike the Colorado Trail, he and Leilani went on their first backpacking trip as a couple in Lost Creek Wilderness. They found cool rocks, took pictures, and got rained on.

Lights Date

december 9, 2017

The months flew by, and Leilani and Nate took turns planning elaborate themed dates for each other. In December, Leilani took Nate on a lights-themed date that included light-up winter mini golf, Denver Zoo lights, and a dinner at Hacienda.

First Anniversary Date

january 19, 2017

For their first anniversary date, Nate decided to recreate their first date with a fancy dinner, winter ice skating in downtown Boulder, and drinking hot chocolate at another scenic night hammocking spot.

Trip to Switzerland

june 2018

Leilani's family invited Nate on a magical trip to Switzerland. They hiked, camped, saw cows, and went paragliding on a whim.

Stress Relief Date

november 17, 2018

After a few rough months for Leilani, Nate pulled off his biggest date caper yet: a stress relief date complete with climbing, relaxing in a salt spa and an infrared sauna, sensory deprivation tanks, dogs, and letting Leilani plaster his face with makeup.

Second Anniversary Date

january 19, 2019

Somehow, they had made it two years without breaking up! Leilani planned an epic second anniversary date with unlimited brazilian food, ice castles, and dogsledding through the Colorado forests.

California

march 22-31, 2019

For spring break, Nate designed a surprise couple vacation to California. Over the course of a week, Nate and Leilani explored redwood forests, watched seals on the pier, hiked on the beach, explored the Monterey Bay Aquarium, and slept in the car to save money.

Big Purchase

august 10, 2019

Nate knew that Leilani was unlike any girl he had ever met, and it was time to lock it down. After a few months of researching and browsing, he secretly ordered an engagement ring!

Dinner with the Parents

september 16, 2019

Before he went for it, Nate wanted to ask Leilani's parents for their blessing, somewhere far away from Samoan war clubs. He asked them out to a private dinner, and it turned out to be a great night. Now, he just had to pop the question!

One More Date

october 4, 2019

Plans were finally in place for the proposal. The day before they left together on a "camping trip" with some good friends, Leilani took Nate on a date to paint pottery and eat some delicious food. She had no idea what the weekend would entail!

The Night Before

october 5, 2019

Nate, Leilani and their two good friends Elsa and Paris all drove to Kebler Pass in search of fall colors. That night, after setting up their tents, Nate and Paris snuck away to scout out the sunrise hike for the next moment and put everything into place.

Engaged!

october 6, 2019

Nate was super nervous when he woke up at 5am for the group's sunrise hike. He stalled on the trail while Paris and Elsa went to set up the signs and lights and flower petals for the big moment. Nate led Leilani to the overlook, told her to open her eyes, and got down on one knee. Everything went perfectly! Paris made breakfast sandwiches afterwards for everyone to celebrate.

The End

Leilani and Nate will be getting married on October 8th, 2020!
